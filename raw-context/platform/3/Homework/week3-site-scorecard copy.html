📍 Week 3 — Site Selection & Lease Strategy

How to score: For each criterion, give an honest score from 1 to 5. There are no bonus points for being optimistic — the value of this exercise is in seeing clearly where the space is strong and where it isn't. Use the notes column to record what you found, what you'd need to fix, and anything you'd need to negotiate.
