90-Day Operations Tracker

Week-by-week operational priorities for your first 90 days after opening. This is Component 9 of your Business Readiness Document.

Week 8 of 8 — Operational Template

How to use this tracker: At the start of each week, read the focus area and note your planned actions. At the end of the week, come back and record what actually happened. The gaps between plan and reality are more valuable than either alone — bring this to any coaching or peer calls you have after opening.

Your Weekly Checklist — Repeat Every Week

The non-negotiable review cadence

Daily

Compare sales to the same day last week. Note anything unusual. Five minutes, every day.

Weekly

Review COGS and labour percentages. Check week-on-week revenue trend. Hold a team debrief.

Monthly

Full P&L vs. Week 2 projections. Menu review. Identify one specific thing to improve next month.
