How to use this template: Fill in the drink name, your planned price, and the estimated cost to make it (ingredients only — coffee, milk, syrups, cup, lid). Menu Summary

Calculating your blended margin: Take your three or four most commonly ordered drinks and estimate what % of your total orders each one represents. Multiply each drink's margin by its share of orders, then add them together. Example: if 40% of orders are lattes (72% margin) and 25% are batch brew (88% margin), those two drinks contribute 0.40×72 + 0.25×88 = 28.8 + 22.0 = 50.8 points toward your blended margin. Do this for your whole menu to get your full blended figure.
