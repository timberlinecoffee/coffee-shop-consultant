Phase 1 — Foundation

Days 1–5  ·  No live service until Day 5

Day 1
Welcome & Concept

Welcome conversation, concept and culture walkthrough, culture document review, bar tour, team introductions, food safety and hygiene basics.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 2
Equipment & Bar Layout

Equipment orientation (espresso machine, grinder, filter setup), bar zone walkthrough, safety and cleaning procedures, POS system basics.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 3
Espresso & Milk

Espresso extraction fundamentals, dialing in, milk steaming to your standard, every milk-based drink on your menu practiced multiple times.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 4
Full Menu & Workflow

Filter and batch coffee, cold drinks, food/pastry items (if applicable), full run-through of the order-to-handoff workflow, opening checklist practice.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 5
Menu Sign-Off & First Live Shadow

Competency check across full menu. First live service — shadow only, observing workflow and guest interaction. Closing checklist. End-of-week debrief.

Notes / Tick when done

Week 1 Check: Can they make every drink on your menu without asking? Do they know the POS, opening, and closing checklists? Have you had an explicit conversation about culture expectations?

Phase 2 — Building Confidence

Days 6–14  ·  On bar with supervision

Day 6–7
Supervised Service

On bar during live service with trainer present. They take orders and make drinks — trainer supports but does not take over. Debrief after each shift.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 8
Rush Handling & Modifications

Practice maintaining workflow during a busy period. How to handle drink modifications, substitutions, and complaints — your specific approach.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 9–10
First Supervised Solo

Trainer on site but not actively on bar. New hire runs the shift. Trainer observes and gives feedback at the end. Not left entirely alone yet.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 11–14
Consolidation & Consistency

Mix of supervised and semi-independent shifts. Focus on consistency of quality, speed, and guest interactions across different shift types and volumes.

Notes / Tick when done

Week 2 Check: Are they holding their standard under pressure? How are they handling guest interactions independently? Is there anything specific that still needs focused attention before moving to independent shifts?

Phase 3 — Independence & Integration

Days 15–30  ·  Independent shifts with check-ins

Day 15
Sign-Off Conversation

Formal conversation: are they ready for fully independent shifts? Review strengths and any remaining development areas. Set expectations for Phase 3.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 16–25
Independent Shifts

Fully independent shifts across different times and volumes. Check in briefly before or after each shift in the first week. Note any patterns in how they handle pressure or service decisions.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 26–29
Development Focus

Identify one or two specific areas to develop further — a technical skill, a guest interaction pattern, or a culture behavior. Set a concrete goal for the 30-day review.

Notes / Tick when done
Day 30
30-Day Review

Formal check-in: performance against training standard, culture alignment, any adjustments needed. Discuss their experience and what support they need going forward.
